About Us

Our Story

We are the Jenkins, a family of four who love eating organic food and supporting our community. We decided to start a food truck that uses locally produced fruits to make delicious smoothies and sell the fruit as well. Our goal is to promote healthy eating habits and help local farmers and businesses thrive. We travel around Carlsbad, California and the beach, offering our fresh and tasty products to customers of all ages. We also donate a portion of our profits to local charities and environmental causes. We are passionate about our food truck and our mission, and we hope to inspire you to join us in making a positive difference in the world.
