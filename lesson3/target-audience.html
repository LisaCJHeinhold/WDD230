Target Audience

Persona 1

Picture

Demographics

"Alex" is a 35-year-old business owner who recently started a company in Hogsmeade.

Motivation

Alex is motivated by a desire to expand their business network and grow their brand.

Alex is interested in joining the Hogsmeade Chamber of Commerce to gain access to networking opportunities and connect with other business owners in the community.

Alex wants to learn about the benefits of joining the chamber, such as marketing and advertising opportunities, access to resources and support, and discounted services.

Alex needs an easy-to-use website that provides a clear overview of the chamber's benefits, membership requirements, and fees. They also want to see success stories from current members and find out how to apply for membership.

Persona 2

Picture

Demographics

"Lily" is a 30-year-old woman who recently moved to the Hogsmeade area with her family.

Motivation

Lily is motivated by a need to settle into her new community and find local services.

Lily is looking for information on local businesses, such as restaurants, shops, and services, and wants to learn about upcoming events and festivals in Hogsmeade.

Lily wants to join the chamber of commerce to connect with local business owners and meet new people in the community.

Lily needs an easy-to-navigate website that provides comprehensive information on local businesses and events, as well as a simple way to join the chamber of commerce and get involved in the community.
